# Consecration

Consecration is all about God saving His people, and His people participating into that.

When will the Lord require us to live the Law of Consecration? Never: He never forced anyone in the past and He will never force anyone now or in the future.
The question isn't _When will the Lord ask me to live it_ , it's _What am I going to do with it?_ ... what are we doing with it?
